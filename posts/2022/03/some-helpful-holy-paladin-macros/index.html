Judgment target’s target

#showtooltip judgment
/assist [@mouseover]
/use judgment
/targetlasttarget

This macro will cast Judgment on your friendly target you have moused over. This is helpful for things like hitting your tank’s target with Judgment of Light if you are talented into it.

Hit closest enemy with Crusader Strike

#showtooltip crusader strike
/cast [harm] crusader strike
/stopmacro [harm]
/targetenemy
/cast crusader strike
/targetlasttarget

This macro will target the nearest enemy and hit it with Crusader Strike, and then target your last target. It seems to be a bit touchy if it re-targets the last target, especially if it’s a target that’s died while hitting the macro.
